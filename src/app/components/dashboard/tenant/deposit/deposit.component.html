How Your Deposit is Protected

Government Protection Scheme

Your deposit is protected under the Kenya Residential Tenancy Act 2023. This means:

  • Your deposit is held in a separate, ring-fenced account
  • It cannot be used by the landlord for other purposes
  • You have access to free dispute resolution services
  • The deposit earns interest at the current government rate

Dispute Resolution Process

If there's a disagreement about your deposit return:

  1. Contact your landlord first to discuss the issue
  2. If unresolved, raise a dispute through the online portal
  3. A neutral mediator will review your case within 14 days
  4. If mediation fails, the case goes to binding arbitration
  5. Final decisions are legally enforceable

Your Rights

  • Right to receive your deposit back within 30 days of lease end
  • Right to receive interest earned on your deposit
  • Right to dispute any deductions from your deposit
  • Right to access all documentation related to your deposit
  • Right to free mediation services for deposit disputes
