1. Introduction

RentEase is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Information Collection

  • Personal Data: Name, email, phone number, property information.

3. How We Use Data

  • To register and authenticate users.

4. Data Sharing

Data may be shared with Landlords, Caretakers, or payment processors for operational purposes. We do not sell your data to third parties.

5. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored securely using encryption and access controls. Escrow accounts for deposits are separately managed.

6. User Rights

Users can request access, correction, or deletion of their personal data. Users can opt out of marketing communications.

7. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies to enhance user experience and track platform usage. No personal data is sold through analytics.
